1. Description of Service

Fud AI is a free, open-source nutrition and fitness tracking application available on iOS and Android. It uses AI to estimate the nutritional content of food from one or more camera or photo-library images analyzed together, iOS Share Extension images, optional meal notes, text descriptions, voice input, iOS Siri Shortcuts / App Intents, and manual entry. It can also look up packaged foods by barcode using Open Food Facts when public product nutrition is available. It includes smart serving units, editable nutrition review, meal what-if previews, food log sorting and customizable meal-time boundaries, customizable Home nutrient cards, decimal nutrient totals, six activity levels, optional nutrient goals, Energy Burn Goals, Adaptive Goals, weight and optional body-fat tracking, optional local water tracking, a workout diary/logger with sets, reps, weight, RPE and calculated burn history, an 873-exercise reference library, AI Coach access to relevant nutrition and workout history, diary export and meal-link sharing, iOS Home/Lock Screen and Android Glance widgets, Apple Watch support on iOS, theme personalization, app-update checks, optional Apple Health / Health Connect integration, and localization in 16 languages on iOS and 15 languages on Android. The app uses Bring Your Own Key access for 13 AI providers, six speech-to-text options, and Apple Intelligence on supported iPhones as a final on-device fallback for eligible food-description analysis. On iOS, an optional Tip Jar lets you support development.

2. AI-Powered Estimates

Nutritional information, Siri food-log estimates, meal what-if suggestions, optional nutrient-goal suggestions, energy-burn goals, adaptive calorie corrections, weight forecasts, calculated workout burns, and Coach responses provided by Fud AI are estimated, not exact. AI-powered estimates are generated by your chosen AI provider or Apple Intelligence on supported iPhones when used as the final fallback for eligible food-description analysis; Adaptive Goals and workout-burn estimates use local calculations from the data you enter. These estimates should be used as general guidance only. Fud AI is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. Water tracking records amounts you enter against the goal you choose; it does not assess hydration or recommend a medically appropriate intake. Always consult a healthcare professional for dietary, hydration, exercise, or weight-related decisions. The daily calorie target, BMR, TDEE, and macro split shown in the app are computed from published equations. Optional detailed goals remain separate from the calorie/protein/carbs/fat calculator. Full citations and source links are available in-app under Settings → Goals & Nutrition → Calculation Methods. Predictive equations and exercise-energy estimates carry inherent error and should not replace personalized clinical assessment.

3. AI Coach Chat Disclaimer

The AI Coach tab is a conversational assistant that can retrieve relevant ranges of your profile, goals, weight, body fat, food log, workout plans, preferences, completed sessions, and logged sets when needed to answer your request. Its replies are generated by the third-party AI provider you select and may be inaccurate, biased, or outdated. Coach is not a substitute for advice from a dietitian, doctor, trainer, physiotherapist, or other qualified professional. Do not rely on Coach for decisions about medical conditions, injuries, eating disorders, pregnancy, or any other health matter that warrants professional guidance.

4. AI Access and API Keys

  • You are responsible for obtaining and managing your own API keys from your chosen AI and speech-to-text providers.
  • API keys are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to us — in iOS Keychain on iOS, in EncryptedSharedPreferences using AES-256 (Android Keystore-backed) on Android.
  • Usage, rate limits, and billing for AI/STT requests are between you and the provider you chose.
  • On supported iPhones, Apple Intelligence may process text, voice-transcribed, and Siri food descriptions on-device only as the final fallback after BYOK provider/fallback attempts fail.

5. Provider Costs

Any usage costs for third-party AI or speech-to-text providers are handled directly by the provider account you configure. Free tiers, credits, pricing, and rate limits are controlled by those providers and may change over time.

6. Third-Party Providers

When you use the app with an AI or STT provider, your requests (including one or more food photos captured or selected together, food photos shared through the iOS Share Extension, meal notes, Siri food descriptions, voice audio, speech-language hints, meal what-if prompts, optional nutrient-goal estimation prompts, Coach messages, and any images attached to Coach) are sent for processing. In BYOK mode, requests go directly from your device to the provider you selected. On supported iPhones, Apple Intelligence may process text, voice-transcribed, and Siri food descriptions on-device as the final fallback after BYOK provider/fallback attempts fail; photo analysis, Coach, and speech transcription continue using the configured AI access path. When you scan a barcode, the barcode value is sent to Open Food Facts for product lookup. Each provider has its own terms and privacy policy that govern how it handles data. You are responsible for reviewing and accepting those terms. We do not control, endorse, or make representations about third-party providers or public product databases.

Tips & legacy Premium

Tips (iOS): The iOS app offers optional consumable Tip Jar purchases (from $0.99 to $9.99, subject to App Store pricing and taxes). Tips are voluntary support for development — they unlock no features and are consumed on purchase. Payment, billing, and refunds are handled by Apple under the App Store's terms. On Android and the web, the optional Ko-fi page serves the same purpose.

Legacy Premium: Earlier iOS versions offered an optional Fud AI Premium subscription for no-key hosted AI access. Premium has been discontinued and is no longer offered; current versions are bring-your-own-key only. If you still have an active Premium subscription from an earlier version, manage or cancel it from your Apple ID subscription settings — deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

Auto-routed providers (OpenRouter openrouter/free): The free-tier OpenRouter model auto-selects an available upstream provider for each request, and that pool can change over time. By selecting openrouter/free you accept that the upstream provider chosen by OpenRouter is outside our control and may change without notice. If you require a fixed upstream, select a specific model ID instead of the auto-routed option.

Barcode lookup: Barcode results depend on Open Food Facts coverage and data quality. If a product is missing or has incomplete nutrition, Fud AI may not be able to create an entry from the barcode; you can instead photograph the product or packaging using Camera or Photos.

Exports and shared meal links: You control where locally generated diary exports are saved or sent. A shared meal link contains the meal names, nutrition, serving details, and emoji in the URL itself so a recipient can preview and import the meal. Anyone with access to the link may read that information. You are responsible for choosing recipients and for avoiding public sharing of information you consider sensitive. Imported meal information remains an estimate and should be reviewed before logging.

Local data and platform backups: Fud AI does not provide its own account or cloud-sync service. Apple and Android may include eligible app data and, for encrypted iOS backups, Keychain items in operating-system backups or device transfers according to your platform settings. Delete All Data clears the current installation but does not modify an older backup already controlled by Apple or Google.

9. Apple Health & Health Connect Integration

By enabling Apple Health (iOS) or Health Connect (Android) integration, you authorize the app to read and write health data as described in our Privacy Policy. Written data includes body measurements, per-meal nutrition, and calculated workout active calories; workout plans and individual set details remain in Fud AI. The app can read authorized body measurements back into your profile and Progress chart. If you enable Energy Burn Goals, it may also read active and total energy burned to estimate calorie targets while keeping macros editable. Where supported, Fud AI can restore records it previously wrote using its own record tags. You can review or revoke permissions through Manage Access in the app's Health settings or the platform's health settings. Delete All Data only wipes local storage — Apple Health / Health Connect samples persist unless you remove them separately.

11. Availability

The app requires an internet connection for hosted AI food analysis, barcode lookup, Coach chat, remote speech-to-text, Android native speech fallback when offline language support is unavailable, and app-update checks. Fully offline AI operation is possible only when you use Ollama with a reachable local endpoint or Apple Intelligence fallback on supported iPhones for eligible food descriptions; Native iOS / Android speech can work offline where the selected language is installed on-device. Offline functionality otherwise includes viewing and editing local nutrition and workout logs, manual entry, water tracking, local reminders and calculations, diary export, browsing the workout library, reading existing Apple Health / Health Connect samples, and re-logging Saved Meals. Some Android OEM skins aggressively limit background alarms; the app exposes a Battery Optimization shortcut in Settings to improve scheduled-reminder reliability, but reminders may still be delayed or dropped by the operating system.
